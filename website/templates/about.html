Our Story

How three developers decided to tackle the browser bloat problem.

The Problem

We were fed up with browsers that come loaded with ads, AI features we never asked for, sponsor content, and enough bloat to sink a ship. Every update seemed to add more stuff we didn't want.

The Solution

So we decided to build our own. Take Chromium (the good parts), strip out all the annoying stuff, and create something that just works. Simple as that.
